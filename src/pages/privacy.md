<p class="text-gray-300 mb-12">
This privacy policy explains how MusiQuiz ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application and website.

<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-6 text-purple-light">MusiQuiz App</h2>

<h3 class="text-lg font-medium mb-3">Data We Collect</h3>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-4">
<strong class="text-white">We do not collect any personal data.</strong> The MusiQuiz app is designed with privacy in mind:
</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside text-gray-300 space-y-2 mb-8">
<li><strong class="text-white">No account required</strong> — Use the app without providing personal information</li>
<li><strong class="text-white">No analytics or tracking</strong> — We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools</li>
<li><strong class="text-white">No advertising</strong> — The app contains no advertisements or ad-related tracking</li>
<li><strong class="text-white">No third-party services</strong> — We do not integrate with third-party services that collect data</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-lg font-medium mb-3">Local Storage</h3>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-4">
The app stores data <strong class="text-white">locally on your device only</strong>:
</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside text-gray-300 space-y-2 mb-4">
<li><strong class="text-white">Game scores and progress</strong> — Your quiz results and achievements</li>
<li><strong class="text-white">Preferences</strong> — Settings such as sound preferences</li>
</ul>
<p class="text-gray-400 text-sm mb-8">
This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.
</p>

<h3 class="text-lg font-medium mb-3">Device Permissions</h3>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside text-gray-300 space-y-2 mb-12">
<li><strong class="text-white">Audio/Microphone</strong> — Used solely for music recognition gameplay. Audio is processed on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-6 text-purple-light">Website</h2>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-12">
This website (<a href="https://musiquiz.pimverlangen.com" class="text-purple-light hover:underline">MusiQuiz</a>) is a static site. It does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind. No personal data is collected, stored, or processed when you visit.
</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-6 text-purple-light">Children's Privacy</h2>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-12">
MusiQuiz is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, there is no children's data to protect beyond the general privacy measures already in place.
</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-6 text-purple-light">Data Security</h2>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-12">
Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our systems. All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures.
</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-6 text-purple-light">Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p class="text-gray-300 mb-12">
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
</p>
